More About Me

I was born in the exuberant city ofJakarta, Indonesia.
I went to school there until university. I graduated in 2004.
After working for around 2.5 years, I moved to Singapore.
My family is in Indonesia.

I moved to Singapore in 2007. I worked there for many years as a Software Engineer. Eventually I became a Singaporean citizen.
But in 2016, I made a dramatic change in my life, I quit my job and left Singapore.

After 5 months in Jakarta, I flew to Toronto, Canada.
To start a new life, leave the IT industry behind, and work for a different industry.
At least, that's what I thought at the time.

In Toronto, I met my husband.
After around 1 year of dating, we got married in 2018.
And in 2019, we had our first child.

During my pregnancy, I decided to return to IT. And I wanted to become a Full-Stack Web Developer.
I found some great courses on Udemy and started from there.
I finished my online bootcamp, completed my demo projects, and picked up some more skills from online sources.

Now I'm ready to work as an IT slave worker again!
